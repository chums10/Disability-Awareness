Quick Legal Note
PHILIPPINE LAW – Batas Pambansa Blg. 344
The Accessibility Law ensures that Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) have equal access.
While it started with physical spaces (ramps, elevators, signage), its principle now extends to digital platforms like websites and apps.
Global Standard – WCAG 2.1
The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) set international rules for digital accessibility. Following WCAG means your site is readable, navigable, and usable for everyone, including people with disabilities.
